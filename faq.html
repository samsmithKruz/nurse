Why are we advised to use the CGFNS CES instead of other Evaluation Body, it takes a longer time with CGFNS.

We advise our client to use the CGFNS CES because after the NCLEX and you hit the immigration phase, you would need to apply for a VisaScreen Certificate with CGFNS, if another Evaluation body was used prior, you would have to pay extra and start your Credential Evaluation from scratch again with CGFNS. This is because only CGFNS offers the VisaScreen Certificate. This will cost more time and money.However, there are client who still opt for other Evaluation bodies such as ERES due to certain reasons that are unique to them. We help such clients also with the Registration.

Why are Nigerians advised to take the NCLEX in Philippines.

Other countries such as the US, UK, CANADA, INDIA, and the likes do not grant Visas to Nigerians on the basis of writing NCLEX. If you are denied a Visa to the US this might affect you during the immigration phase, at the level of the USCIS, so we advise clients to take the test in the Philippines. There is 100 percent assurance you would get the Philippine visa for NCLEX, it is fast and very affordable. It is best to save yourself the stress of thinking through the reason for a denied visa and how to apply again with other countries.

After self-sponsoring myself for the NCLEX, is it still important I apply to an Agency for the remaining Phase.

Yes it is. For you to be issued an Employment Based Visa or a Green card to work as a Nurse in the USA, a Nurse Recruitment Agency or US based Hospital has to file the i140 Petition on your behalf with the USCIS. You cannot do this on your own.

How fast is the remaining phase of the process after passing the NCLEX.

Well this is influenced by a lot of factors we cannot control most of the time. The major delay Nurses tend to face after passing the NCLEX is with the USCIS. Denials in your petition, Request For Evidence and the likes can pose a delay in your process, also the NVC stage can be real slow sometimes. Priority Date could also cause a delay, but be rest assured we are not affected by this, at least not for now as our PD is current. Delays can also be from the part of the agencies, could be they don’t have a job placement for you yet, or there is a policy you finish a particular transition course before deployment and you are behind. A lot of things could cause a delay, but the most common is the USCIS. This is the toughest stage of the whole immigration process, after crossing this stage, it’s always a smooth sail with the other process.

What Agency would you advise a self-sponsored Nurse to sign up with after the NCLEX.

Well, no agency is perfect; every agency has its own pros and cons. Your choice of an agency should be dependent on what you place as priority amongst a list of other things. There are different contract terms with Agencies and the best we can do as a company when you get a Consult is familiarize you with all that is at stake based on our firsthand experience with them so far. This is the best you can get and it is all you need. In the end you get to make the decision yourself, but be sure it would be a Well-Informed one.

When can I apply to be recruited by an agency again, after I was turned down

This is dependent on the reason you were not recruited. Some agencies even tell you when you should apply again based on their assessment of you and when they feel you could meet their requirement. However, Sajpointe Academy advices you seek proper consult before you submit an application to any US recruitment agency, to avoid such delays as this and also give you a better chance of being recruited.
